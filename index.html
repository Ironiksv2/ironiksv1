Welcome to Saint John, New Brunswick

Discover the beauty, history, and vibrant community of Saint John, Canada’s oldest incorporated city. Whether you’re visiting for the stunning harbor views, the historic architecture, or the lively CB radio community, there’s something here for everyone.

Places to Visit

Saint John Harbor

Explore the bustling harbor, where you can catch a glimpse of the city’s maritime heritage.

Reversing Falls Rapids

Witness the incredible Reversing Falls, where the tides of the Bay of Fundy reverse the flow of the Saint John River.

King’s Square

Relax in King’s Square, a historic park in the heart of Uptown Saint John.
